Our Love Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is our favorite.

First Meeting

It was a rainy afternoon in September 2015 when we first met at a coffee shop. Dimas spilled his coffee on Gita's favorite book, and instead of getting angry, she laughed it off. That moment sparked a conversation that lasted for hours.

First Date

Two weeks later, we had our first official date at a small Italian restaurant. The food was mediocre, but the company was unforgettable. We talked until the restaurant closed, and then walked through the city streets until sunrise.

The Proposal

On a trip to Paris in 2022, Dimas proposed at the top of the Eiffel Tower at sunset. Gita said yes before he could even finish his sentence. It was a magical moment that we'll cherish forever.
