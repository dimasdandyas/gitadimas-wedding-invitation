Our Love Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is our favorite.

First Meeting

Our story began in 2015, back in high school. Dimas first saw Gita standing on the school field — a moment that felt like time stood still. Captivated by her presence, he turned to one of her classmates and softly asked, 'What's the name of that girl?'

First Date

On March 6, 2016, we had our first date — we watched Zootopia and had lunch at Es Teler 77. After the date, Dimas told Gita how he felt about her, and that's where it all started.

The Proposal

On April 5, 2025, Dimas, accompanied by his family, visited Gita’s home to respectfully ask her parents for their blessing to propose.
